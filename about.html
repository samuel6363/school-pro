About Our School

Star Matriculation Higher Secondary School, A high class school with traditional environment is designed with a Moto of learning for life from the Sourceful thought of Swami Vivekananda "Man Making Education".The founder and Correspondent Mr.C.Vijayakumar,B.B.M., has gifted this school for the learning community with an innovative dream 'Stress less Learning" in 1997.

He framed the portfolio of the school with the Blessings of Almighty and soulful support of trio Mr.Krishnan.,M.A.,M.Ed., Mr.B.Selvadurai.,B.A.,B.T., and Prof.R.Pandiaraj M.A.,P.G.D.T.E., with the precious blessings of late Krishnan other two paragons are always with the school by extending their valuable suggestions and guidance.
